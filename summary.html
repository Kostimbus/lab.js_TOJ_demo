Attention experiment

Please prepare yourselves for the beginning of the Experiment!

You will two circles and a fixation cross in the middle. Please try to focus at the cross during the whole experiment. Each of the circles will flash once with a little latency. Based on this, your task is to decide which of the two has flashed first. Left mouse button for the left one and right for the right one respectively.

By pressing the space bar, you'll start the practice trials.
